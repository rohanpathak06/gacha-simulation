How It Works

Rarity Tiers

5★ Legendary & Mythic — rarest drops
4★ Epic & Rare — uncommon finds
3★ Common & Uncommon — standard pulls

Pity System

Every pull without a 5★ increases your pity counter. Once you reach 90 pulls, a 5★ is guaranteed. Soft pity starts at 74 pulls, gradually raising your odds.

Controls

Pull ×1 Single pull — one item at a time
Pull ×10 Multi-pull — ten items at once
Reset Clears all history and starts fresh
